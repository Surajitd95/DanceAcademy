About Us

A Legacy of Grace & Devotion

Founded in the heart of Durgapur, Nritya Kala Mandir has been nurturing the art of Indian classical dance for over 19 years. Our institute is a sanctuary where tradition meets passion, and every movement tells a story passed down through generations.

Our Guru, a graded artist of Doordarshan Kendra Kolkata, has performed across prestigious Indian festivals and internationally in Germany, Netherlands, and Sweden — bringing world-class expertise to every class.
